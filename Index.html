Timothy Edwards middle school

Timothy Edwards Middle School, located in South Windsor, Conn., is part of the South Windsor Public Schools. It offers classes to students in grades six through eight and has an enrollment of more than 1,300. It provides an educational environment where students can discover and use their full potential and addresses the needs and interests of students during the transition between elementary and high school. It provides a comprehensive educational program, including core subjects, such as mathematics, language arts, social studies and science. Courses are also offered to all students, which include computer education, library, health and physical education. All students are encouraged to take part in the school newspaper and student council. Timothy Edwards Middle School offers a variety of sports, including football, basketball, track and swimming

School Library

The mission of the school library media program is to ensure that students and staff are effective users of ideas and information. The school library media specialist (SLMS) empowers students to be: • critical thinkers, • enthusiastic readers, • skillful researchers, and • ethical users of information. --Excerpted from Empowering Learners: Guidelines for School Library Media Programs by the Association of School Librarians, a division of the American Library Association, copyright 2009 American Library Association.
